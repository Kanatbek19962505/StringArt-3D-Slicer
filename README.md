🏷️ Proposed Names
G-Weaver 3D AL-JIBAL

📝 Program Description
[Name] is a specialized G-code generator that transforms 2D raster images into intricate String Art masterpieces using a standard 3D printer.

Unlike traditional slicers that build 3D objects layer-by-layer, this software calculates a continuous, non-breaking path of plastic filament that stretches across a frame. By strategically overlapping thousands of lines, the algorithm creates varying densities of material that emerge as high-contrast portraits and complex geometric patterns. It supports multi-color CMYK processing, built-in image enhancement, and automated perimeter "anchor" frames for perfect bed adhesion.

🚀 Applications
1. Bespoke Interior Decor

Creating high-end, tactile wall art and personalized portraits for modern homes and offices.

Producing unique, semi-transparent window hangings that play with natural light.

2. Brand Activation & Marketing

Live "Art-in-Progress" demonstrations at tech expos or gallery openings to attract crowds.

Producing branded corporate gifts that showcase innovation and craftsmanship.

3. STEM & Computer Science Education

Visualizing path-finding algorithms and the mathematical principles of the Bresenham's line algorithm.

Teaching students how to manipulate G-code for non-standard manufacturing.

4. Fashion & Textile Design

Printing decorative, flexible patterns directly onto mesh fabrics or garments using TPU (flexible) filaments.

Designing "optical lace" structures for avant-garde accessories.

5. Rapid Prototyping of Grids

Engineering custom filters or screens with variable density based on visual or mathematical inputs.
